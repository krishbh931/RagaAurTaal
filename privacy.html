Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Raga Aur Taal collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website or contact us.

Our website is hosted by Hostinger. When you visit our site, Hostinger may automatically collect standard server log data, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and the time of your visit. This information helps the website function properly and remain secure.

We believe in data minimization. We do not personally manage or store large user databases, but we use trusted third-party services that may process information when you interact with the site.

Contact Form

Our contact form is powered by Google Forms. When you submit an inquiry, the information you enter, such as your name, email address, phone number, and message, is processed by Google Forms and made available to us so we can respond to your inquiry.

Direct Communication Buttons

If you contact us through email, phone, SMS/text message, or WhatsApp, we may receive the contact details, phone number, profile information, message content, or other information you choose to send through those services.

Our website links to the official Raga Aur Taal YouTube channel. If you click that link, you will be redirected to YouTube, which is operated by Google and governed by Google’s own privacy practices.

We have not intentionally coded first-party advertising cookies into this website. However, third-party tools embedded or linked through the site, including Google Forms and YouTube, may use cookies, tracking technologies, or similar tools for account authentication, spam prevention, analytics, video engagement, security, or platform functionality.

You can control, block, clear, or manage cookies through your browser privacy settings.

4. How We Use Information

We use information you provide only to respond to your inquiry, message, collaboration request, performance-related communication, or other direct communication with Raga Aur Taal.

5. Data Sharing and Protection

We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your contact information to third-party marketing brokers. Your communication information is used only for the purposes described in this policy and may be processed through the third-party platforms you choose to use, such as Google Forms, email, SMS, phone, WhatsApp, YouTube, or our hosting provider.

6. Sensitive Information

Please do not submit sensitive personal, financial, medical, legal, or confidential information through the contact form, email, SMS/text message, WhatsApp, or other communication links on this website.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request removal of contact information you previously sent us, please contact us directly.
